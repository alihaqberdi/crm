SEO

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. It is the process of improving the visibility and ranking of a website or web page in search engine results pages (SERPs) such as Google, Bing, and Yahoo. The goal of SEO is to drive organic (unpaid) traffic to a website by improving its visibility and ranking for relevant search queries.
